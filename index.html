My Trip to Morocco

I would love to travel to Morocco one day. It is a country full of colours, beautiful buildings, and friendly people. I want to explore the markets in Marrakesh and see all the handmade items, spices and carpets. I imagine walking through the busy streets, trying mint tea, and hearing music in the main square. I also want to visit the blue city of Chefchaouen and take lots of photos of the blue houses and steps. Another dream of mine is to ride a camel in the Sahara Desert and watch the sunset over the sand dunes. Morocco has so many interesting places, and I think this trip would be unforgettable.
